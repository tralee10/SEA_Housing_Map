Seattle Housing Permit & Supply Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes housing permits and new housing supply in Seattle from 2005-2025 using proportional symbols. You can also explore population patterns using the 2010 census map. Dot sizes indicate the number of permits or units in an area, while census tracts show population density by color. Click on any dot or tract to view detailed information about permits or population.

Filter permits by year, permit type, dwelling type, neighborhood, or urban village. The dynamic chart updates automatically to show the top locations for new housing units based on your selections.
